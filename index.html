Patch 8.3

Patch 8.3 is the third major content update of Battle for Azeroth. The update was announced on October 7th 2019, and will include several new features, and new content, like a new raid, the mechagnome and vulpera allied races, revamps to the auctionhouse and a new PvP season. A release date is yet to be set, but the patch is avaiable for testing on the Public Test Realm.

Phase 3

Phase 3 is the 2nd major content update for World of Waarcraft: Classic. The patch was released on December 10th 2019. The content update included important features such as PvP battlegrounds, which introduce a significant change in the Honor system, shifting the combat away from the open world and rather in specific PvP instances, a welcome change for many players. The raid "Blackwing Lair" also opens, allowing for the collection of new gear, and elementium ore, needed to craft the legendary weapon, "Thunderfury, Blessed Blade of The Windseeker".
