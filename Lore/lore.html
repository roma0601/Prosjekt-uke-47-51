The Second War

The Seoncd War, often called The second Great War, is one of the biggest conflicts between the horde and alliance. The war had many consequences for both the horde and the alliance. The war was startet directly after the end of the First War and was initiated by the orcish Warchief Orgrim Doomhammer, in his quest to conquer all of Azeroth to give a home to the orcish Horde.
